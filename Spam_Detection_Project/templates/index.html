Security Tips

🚫 Unknown Links

Never click links from unknown or suspicious messages.

🔐 Personal Info

Do not share OTP, passwords, or bank details via SMS.

📩 Fake Offers

Be careful with messages promising free rewards or prizes.

⚠️ Verification

Always verify messages from banks or services through official apps.
